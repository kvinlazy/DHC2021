IMPORTANT!

Dear prospective authors presenters and attendees of the 17th Symposium of DHC 2020,

After considering all the options, IEA DHC and the organizer of the 17th Symposium of DHC2020 in Nottingham,

decided to postpone the 17th International Symposium on DHC in Nottingham to September between 5th – 9th, 2021.

We agreed that it makes little sense to keep the dates as planned for the 17th Symposium of DHC in September 2020 due to the implications of the COVID-19 pandemic has on international travel. Even if all restrictive measures were lifted before the symposium, there would still be not enough time to plan the trip for many people.

Also the motivation for authors to write a paper by May 2020 as currently expected of the presenters is probably rather low due to the uncertainties involved.

The new dates of the Symposium and the new deadlines for submission and registration in 2021 will be announced by September 5th 2020.

Thank you for your consideration.

DHC Team


The 17th International Symposium on District Heating and Cooling is the world’s gathering place for district heating and cooling experts from academia and industry. It is first time taking place in UK. It is where new networks are built, and latest knowledge is shared: in the event halls, in and around sessions, along with the industry tour and in various special events. The 17th International Symposium on District Heating and Cooling focus on solutions, innovations and best-practice examples that make district energy a cornerstone of a sustainable energy future.
